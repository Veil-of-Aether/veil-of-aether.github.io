About The Veil of Aether

The Veil of Aether is a free and open-source (FOSS) action-adventure RPG. It is based in the fictional land of Neyvara, a world centered around the four elements of Earth, Fire, Wind, and Water. The protagonist of the game is Darian (the player), a young adult who sets out on an epic adventure to save the world from an evil sorcerer. He must fight through trials and dungeons to reach the final climactic battle.
